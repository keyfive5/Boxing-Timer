# Privacy Policy — Boxing Rounds

**Effective date:** July 2, 2026

Boxing Rounds is a boxing round timer app for iOS.

## Data Collection

Boxing Rounds does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Period.

- No account or sign-up is required.
- No analytics or tracking of any kind.
- No advertising.
- No third-party services or SDKs that collect data.
- The app does not connect to the internet at all.

Your timer settings (number of rounds, round length, rest length, sound and vibration preferences) are saved only on your device so the app remembers them between launches. This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.
